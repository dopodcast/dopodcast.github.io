Show 2: A Globally Distributed Team

Oct 18th, 2013 | Comments

An interview with Andrew Hooker (github, twitter) and Ryan Bigg (github, twitter) of Spree Commerce, where we discuss the implications of working inside of a globally distributed team, and on a set of products which comprise a large open source component.

MP3 Download, RSS Subscribe

Topics covered:

  • How Spree is coordinated and features are decided
  • Sprint planning across time zones
  • GitHub workflow
  • Spree team structure
  • How teams work across great distances

Do: A Podcast About Ruby and Rubyists

Sep 28th, 2013 | Comments

“do” is a podcast about Ruby, Rubyists and their culture. We hope to grow to be a regular (at this stage, two-weekly) show, with a rotating panel of up to 10 people.

Get involved.

The show schedule is managed in our GitHub issues, ideas for future shows can also be raised as issues for discussion, and may wind up being assigned to the schedule (issue milestones) for future recording.

Here’s how you can get involved:

  • If you’d like to be on a panel, add yourself to the panel list of an upcoming show. no more than 5 panelists, plus guest per show please!

  • If you’d like to be present at the recording of one of our episodes? Just follow us on Google+ and we’ll post events for our upcoming hangouts-on-air.

  • Make show or interview suggestions by raising or commentint on our GitHub issues.
